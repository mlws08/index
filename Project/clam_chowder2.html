Clam Chowder

Jump to Recipe

Ingredients

Stock

  • 30 Shelled Clams
  • 1 lb Raw Shrimp
  • 4 Cups Seafood Stock

Chowder

  • 1/2 Cup Bacon, chopped
  • 1 1/2 Cup Onion, diced
  • 2 Cups Red/Yellow Potatoes, diced
  • 3/4 Stick of Butter
  • 3/4 Cup of Flour
  • 1 Tbsp of Clam Base
  • 4 Cups Seafood Stock
  • 4 Cups Chicken Stock
  • 1 Tbsp Thyme
  • 1 Tbsp Parsley
  • 1 Tsp Rosemary
  • 1 Tsp Red Pepper Flakes
  • 1 Cup of Heavy Cream
  • 1/2 Cup of Cheese

Instructions

  1. Bring 4 cups of seafood stock to a boil in a dutch oven, then add clams. Reduce heat to a simmer, cover and cook for 8-10 mins. Once clams have fully opened, remove from dutch oven and set aside.
  2. Add shrimp to stock, and boil for 1-2 mins. Then set aside.
  3. Strain stock and reserve. Wipe out dutch oven.
  4. In dutch oven, fully cook bacon. Remove and set aside. Reserve approx. 2 Tbsp on bacon grease.
  5. Add bacon grease to dutch oven. Next, add onions and cook until soft. Next add potatoes and cook until tender. Remove and set aside.
  6. After cooking the bacon and vegetables, melt butter. Next add flour and clam base. Cook together to form a rue, approx. 2-5 minutes.
  7. Next, stir in seafood & chicken stocks. Stir periodically, allow stock mixture to ticken.
  8. Add bacon, ontions, and potatoes.
  9. Add Thyme, Rosemary, and Red Pepper Flakes
  10. Stir in cheese and heavy cream. Next add clams and shrimp. Cover and simmer for 10 minutes.
  11. Serve with shredded cheese, green onion, and saltines.
